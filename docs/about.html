Our story

We know the problem
because we lived it.

no~bull consulting grew out of a simple frustration. Running a small consulting business means wearing every hat — accountant, project manager, salesperson, administrator. The tools that exist to help with this are almost all built for someone else.

The large accounting platforms assume you have an accountant to configure them and a budget that scales with your growth. The cheaper alternatives cut so many corners that they create more work than they save. And most of them hold your data hostage — cancel your subscription and your financial history goes with it.

"Your data should be yours. Your tools should respect your time. Your software should cost what it's worth to you — not what the market can bear."

We started building our own tools on Google Workspace — a platform that most UK small businesses already use, trust, and pay for. Google Sheets is not a compromise. It is a powerful, flexible, auditable data store that your accountant can access, that you can export instantly, and that you own completely.

no~bull books was the first product. Built from the ground up for UK compliance — VAT, MTD, HMRC integration, Corporation Tax — not retrofitted from an American base. And we are just getting started.
