Common questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I need a Google account?
Yes. no~bull books runs on Google Apps Script and stores your data in a Google Sheet in your Google Drive. A free Google account or Google Workspace account is required.
Is no~bull books MTD compliant?
no~bull books has applied for HMRC recognition for MTD for VAT and MTD ITSA. The software is built to HMRC’s API specifications and submits returns directly. We will confirm recognition status on this page once granted.
Can I import my data from Xero or QuickBooks?
Yes. no~bull books includes a full migration facility for Xero, QuickBooks and FreeAgent — chart of accounts, trial balance, invoices, bills and contacts, typically in under an hour.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data stays in your Google Drive, intact, forever. It’s just a Google Sheet you already own — no export process, no fees, no deletion.
Can my accountant access my books?
Yes. Add your accountant with the Accountant role. They get read/write access to financial data and can submit MTD returns on your behalf, without access to your settings or user management.
Does no~bull books work on mobile?
Yes. no~bull books is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets. Create invoices, check your P&L and submit VAT returns from any device.
Do I need to understand double-entry bookkeeping?
No. All journal postings happen automatically when you create invoices, record payments and approve bills. You describe what happened — the software handles the accounting.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — 30 days, no credit card required. You get full Pro plan access during your trial. At the end, choose a plan or your account pauses. Your data is never deleted.
