About no~bull consulting
What is no~bull consulting?

no~bull consulting is a UK software company (trading name of Edward Jenkins) that builds practical, Google-native business applications. We develop the no~bull suite of SaaS tools — starting with no~bull books (accounting) and no~bull visa (Spanish visa case management) — as well as bespoke custom applications.

Where is my data stored?

All workspace data for both no~bull books and no~bull visa is stored in a Google Sheets spreadsheet within your own Google Drive. We do not copy or store your data on our own servers. If you ever stop using our software, your data remains in your Google Drive — it’s just a spreadsheet you already own.

How does sign-in work?

Solicitors and staff sign in using their existing Google account via Google OAuth — no new password to manage. End clients (visa applicants) receive a secure magic-link email that signs them in with one click. Sessions last 30 days on a given device, so day-to-day use just requires bookmarking the app URL.

no~bull books
What is no~bull books?

no~bull books is cloud-based accounting software for UK sole traders and micro-businesses, built for HMRC Making Tax Digital compliance. Features include direct MTD VAT submission, professional invoicing with PDF generation and email delivery, bank reconciliation, multi-currency support, Corporation Tax computation (CT600), and Self Assessment (SA103/ITSA). Priced at £15/month with a 30-day free trial.

Is no~bull books HMRC MTD compatible?

Yes. no~bull books supports direct MTD VAT submission via the HMRC API and is built for MTD compliance from the ground up. Our full HMRC MTD compatibility statement is available on the site.

How much does no~bull books cost?

£15 per month on the Standard plan, which includes all features. A 30-day free trial is available with no credit card required. You can cancel at any time.

no~bull visa
What is no~bull visa?

no~bull visa is a case management application purpose-built for solicitors handling Spanish visa applications. It covers the full workflow from initial client intake through to visa decision. Features include a client portal for document upload, document checklists per visa type, stage tracking, invoicing, family and dependant application management, in-app messaging with email notifications, and a management information dashboard with SLA monitoring.

Which Spanish visa types does no~bull visa support?

no~bull visa supports the Digital Nomad Visa (DNV), Non-Lucrative Visa (NLV), Student Visa, Golden Visa, Work Visa, and post-arrival TIE (residence card) applications. Document checklists and stage workflows are configured per visa type. Solicitors can also customise visa types and checklists to match their practice.

How much does no~bull visa cost?

£39/month for the Solo plan (single solicitor practice) or £49/month for the Practice plan (multi-staff firms). Both plans include all features and a 30-day free trial with no credit card required.

Is no~bull visa GDPR compliant?

Yes. no~bull visa is designed for compliance with both UK GDPR and EU GDPR. The subscribing solicitor is the data controller for their clients’ case data; no~bull consulting acts as the data processor. Case data is stored in the solicitor’s own Google Drive. The EU adequacy decision for the UK means data flows between EU-based solicitors (e.g. in Spain) and the platform are lawful without additional transfer mechanisms. See our full privacy policy.

Can a solicitor based in Spain use no~bull visa?

Yes. no~bull visa is used by both UK-based and Spain-based solicitors. The platform operates under UK GDPR and EU GDPR. The adequacy decision granted by the European Commission means data can flow freely between the EU and UK without additional safeguards.
