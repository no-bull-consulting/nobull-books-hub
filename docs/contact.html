Common questions

Before you get in touch.

Do I need a Google account to use no~bull books?

Yes — no~bull books uses your Google account for secure login and to store your data in Google Sheets. You do not need a Gmail address; any email address can be linked to a Google account.

Can I try it before committing?

Yes. Every new account starts with a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. Cancel at any time with no charge.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is in your own Google Sheet, in your own Google account. If you cancel, you keep everything. We have no ability to delete or withhold your data.

Do you work with accountants?

Yes — our Accountant plan is designed for practices managing multiple clients. Each client gets their own instance, and you manage all of them from a single dashboard.

Can you customise no~bull books for my specific needs?

Yes. We offer customisation services for clients with specific workflow requirements. Get in touch to discuss what you need and we will tell you whether it is possible and what it would cost.

How long does a bespoke project take?

Most projects we build fall in the range of 4-12 weeks depending on complexity. We always agree a timeline before starting and deliver in milestones so you can see progress throughout.
