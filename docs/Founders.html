The exchange

What we ask in return

This isn't a free-for-all. We're looking for businesses willing to engage with us properly — not just take six months free and disappear. In exchange for the founding member terms, we ask three things:

  • 01
    One scheduled feedback call per quarter
    A 30-minute conversation with Edward, four times a year. Honest feedback on what's working, what's frustrating, and what should come next. This is the part that matters most to us.
  • 02
    A public case study or testimonial by month 6
    Your choice of format — a written case study, a short video, or a LinkedIn post. We'll make it easy and we'll send you a draft to approve.
